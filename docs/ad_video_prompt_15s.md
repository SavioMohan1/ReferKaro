# ReferKaro 15-Second Ad Video AI Generation Prompt

This is a high-impact, fast-paced 15-second version of the ad prompt. It is optimized for short-form platforms (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts) and designed for modern AI video generators (Sora, Runway Gen-3 Alpha, Kling).

---

## 🎬 15-Second Storyboard & Voiceover Script

### Beat 1: The Pain (0:00 - 0:05)
* **Visual:** A split-second shot of a candidate getting another automated "application rejected" email, followed by a rapid zoom-out into a digital data void.
* **On-Screen Text (Neon Red):** "GHOSTED BY ATS?"
* **Voiceover:** "Tired of your resume disappearing into a black hole?"

### Beat 2: The Solution (0:05 - 0:10)
* **Visual:** Sudden glitch transition into a glowing, dark-mode dashboard. A 3D key-shaped token slots into a field, generating a glowing secure proxy email: `ref-xxxx@referkaro.app`. An employee matches it and clicks **"Refer"**.
* **On-Screen Text (Neon Cyan):** "GET AN EMPLOYEE REFERRAL. SECURE & VERIFIED."
* **Voiceover:** "Get referred directly by verified employees. Secure, token-backed, and 100% private."

### Beat 3: Call to Action (0:10 - 0:15)
* **Visual:** The camera zooms out showing a mobile screen with the ReferKaro dashboard inbox saying **"Referral Confirmed!"** with a green checkmark. Fades to logo card.
* **On-Screen Text (Gradient):** "REFERKARO. Get Referred, Not Rejected. referkaro.app"
* **Voiceover:** "Stop applying. Start referring. Join ReferKaro today."

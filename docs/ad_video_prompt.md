## 🎨 Creative & Aesthetic Direction

* **Visual Style:** Ultra-sleek cyberpunk-corporate, hyper-modern, clean technology aesthetics. Dark glassmorphism interfaces, smooth neon glows, and micro-interactions.
* **Color Palette:**
  * Primary Background: Deep obsidian navy (`#050A14`)
  * Primary Glow: Electric neon cyan (`#00F0FF`)
  * Secondary Glow: Cyber purple/violet (`#7B5EFF`)
  * Text/UI Elements: Pure white and frosted glass overlays
* **Typography:** Bold sans-serif UI titles (Syne/Outfit font styles), clean and dynamic motion graphics.
* **Core Message:** "Monetize the Referral Attempt. No more black holes. Get referred, not rejected."
